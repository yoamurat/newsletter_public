BP Sells 265,000bpd Gelsenkirchen Refinery to Klesch GroupDue Diligence

BP agreed to divest its integrated Gelsenkirchen refinery complex to independent European refiner Klesch Group, processing 12 million tonnes/year crude oil. The deal includes Bottrop tank farm, DHC Solvent Chemie subsidiary, and related logistics assets, employing 1,800 people. Transaction expected to close H2 2026 pending regulatory approvals.

Klesch Group will require comprehensive technical due diligence on the integrated refining and petrochemical operations, plus brownfield FEED studies for operational optimisation
